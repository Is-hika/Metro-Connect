Explore Delhi's Iconic Attractions

Discover the rich cultural heritage and modern marvels of Delhi with convenient metro access to all major tourist spots.

Red Fort

The Red Fort is a historic fort in Delhi that served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors for nearly 200 years. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is made of red sandstone and is an iconic symbol of India's rich history.

Nearest Metro: Chandni Chowk (Yellow Line) - 1.2km walk
Timings: 9:30 AM - 4:30 PM (Closed Mondays)
Best Time: Oct-Mar

Qutub Minar

At 73 meters tall, Qutub Minar is the world's tallest brick minaret and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Built in 1193, this magnificent tower is surrounded by ancient ruins that showcase Delhi's medieval architecture.

Nearest Metro: Qutub Minar (Yellow Line) - 500m walk
Timings: 7:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Daily)
Best Time: Oct-Mar

India Gate

India Gate is a war memorial dedicated to 82,000 soldiers of the British Indian Army who died in World War I. This 42-meter tall arch stands at the center of a hexagonal complex with fountains and lush lawns.

Nearest Metro: Central Secretariat (Yellow Line) - 1.5km walk
Timings: Open 24 hours
Best Time: Evening

Lotus Temple

The Lotus Temple is a Bahá'í House of Worship notable for its flowerlike shape. Completed in 1986, it has become one of Delhi's most visited buildings, welcoming people of all faiths to pray and meditate.

Nearest Metro: Kalkaji Mandir (Magenta Line) - 1km walk
Timings: 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM (Closed Mondays)
Best Time: Oct-Mar

Akshardham Temple

Swaminarayan Akshardham is a Hindu temple complex that displays traditional Hindu and Indian culture, spirituality, and architecture. The complex features exhibitions, musical fountains, and boat rides through India's history.

Nearest Metro: Akshardham (Blue Line) - Direct access
Timings: 9:30 AM - 6:30 PM (Closed Mondays)
Best Time: Oct-Mar

Humayun's Tomb

Humayun's Tomb is the tomb of the Mughal Emperor Humayun and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Built in 1570, it was the first garden-tomb on the Indian subcontinent and inspired several architectural innovations.

Nearest Metro: JLN Stadium (Violet Line) - 1.8km walk
Timings: Sunrise to Sunset (Daily)
Best Time: Oct-Mar
