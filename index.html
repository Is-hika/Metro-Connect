Delhi Metro FAQs

Q. What items are not allowed in Delhi Metro?

A. The list of prohibited items in Delhi Metro includes:

  • Sharp Objects: Knives, swords, meat cleavers, etc.
  • Explosive Materials: Gunpowder, fireworks, hand grenades, plastic explosives, etc.
  • Inflammable Items: Paints, petroleum, aerosols, wet batteries, etc.
  • Offensive Items: Blood, carcasses of dead animals, unsealed raw meat/fish, plants/saplings, skeletons, bones, rags, etc.
  • Pets: Birds and other animals.

The list is updated periodically by Delhi Metro.

Q. How much luggage is allowed in Delhi Metro?

A. Regular Metro Lines: One bag containing personal belongings is allowed, with dimensions not exceeding 80 cm x 50 cm x 30 cm and a maximum weight of 25 kg. Baggage in the form of bundles is not permitted.

Airport Express Line: Two bags containing personal belongings are allowed, with dimensions not exceeding 90 cm x 75 cm x 45 cm and a maximum weight of 32 kg. Baggage in the form of bundles is not permitted.

Q. What are the operational hours of Delhi Metro?

A. The Delhi Metro operates from approximately 5:30 AM to 11:30 PM, with the first and last train times varying slightly by line and station. On Sundays and holidays, services may start a little later.

The Airport Express Line operates from 4:45 AM to 11:30 PM.

Q. Are there any discounts available for frequent travelers?

A. Yes, Delhi Metro offers several smart card options with discounted fares:

  • Regular Smart Card: 10% discount on every ride
  • Student Smart Card: 20% discount for students with valid ID
  • Senior Citizen Smart Card: 30% discount for senior citizens (women above 58 years and men above 60 years)
  • Tourist Smart Card: Unlimited travel for 1 or 3 days
Q. Why is food not allowed in Delhi Metro?

A. Eating is not allowed in Delhi Metro to maintain cleanliness in trains and at stations. Passengers can carry food, but they are not permitted to consume it during their journey. Officials monitor this at stations and on trains. Most interchange metro stations have cafes and food outlets where passengers can sit and eat.

Q. Can I eat or drink inside the Delhi Metro?

A. No, eating and drinking are prohibited inside Delhi Metro trains and stations. Announcements are regularly made to remind passengers of this rule.

Q. Can I carry alcohol, tobacco, or cigarettes in Delhi Metro?

A. Delhi Metro allowed drug items as per following instructions: Alcohol: Two sealed bottles of alcohol are allowed per passenger. Cigarettes/Tobacco: One packet of cigarettes and one lighter or matchbox per passenger is permitted. While carrying alcohol, tobacco, and cigarettes is allowed, consumption of these items inside the Delhi Metro is strictly prohibited.
